Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a wide range of auto detailing services including interior and exterior detailing, paint correction, ceramic coating, and even furniture cleaning. Our goal is to restore and protect your vehicle's appearance.
The duration of our services varies depending on the package and the condition of your vehicle. A basic detailing service might take 2-3 hours, while more comprehensive services like paint correction or ceramic coating can take a full day or more.
Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your vehicle's paint, creating a protective layer. It offers long-lasting protection against UV rays, chemical stains, and etching, while also making your car easier to clean and enhancing its gloss. It's an excellent investment for those looking to maintain their vehicle's appearance for years to come.
We recommend having your car professionally detailed every 4-6 months to maintain its appearance and protect its value. However, this can vary based on factors such as your driving habits, where you park your car, and local weather conditions. Regular maintenance washes between detailing sessions are also important.
